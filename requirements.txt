Functional Requirements
Monitor SLA status of all incidents and service requests in real time.
Predict potential SLA breaches using historical and current ticket data.
Notify assigned agents and managers before SLA deadlines.
Provide AI-generated breach justifications based on ticket history and activity logs.
Allow agents to review, edit, and submit justifications.
Maintain an audit trail of SLA breaches, notifications, and justifications.
Generate dashboards and reports for SLA compliance and breach analysis.
Integrate with ITSM platforms (e.g., ServiceNow, Jira, BMC Remedy).
Non-Functional Requirements
High availability (99.9% uptime).
Real-time response with low notification latency.
Secure access using role-based authentication and data encryption.
Scalable architecture to handle thousands of tickets simultaneously.
Reliable logging, backup, and recovery mechanisms.
User-friendly virtual agent interface with natural language support.
Compliance with organizational security and data privacy policies.
Easy integration with existing enterprise systems through APIs.
